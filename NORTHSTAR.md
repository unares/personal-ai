# ONETHING NORTHSTAR

> Do One Thing. Earn Full Autonomy.

Edit this file to define your long-term vision for onething.

## What Is An Entity

An entity is your portfolio or venture — a domain of focus where you apply the "Do One Thing" principle. Each entity has its own vault, its own agents, and its own northstar.

## Agent Hierarchy

- **Clark** (Clarity Architect) — your philosophical brain. Holds the long-term vision, surfaces patterns, asks hard questions. Read-only.
- **AIOO** (AI Operating Officer) — your operational brain. Drives execution, maintains this northstar, spawns App Builders. Full vault access.
- **App Builder** — your hands. Builds one thing at a time, scoped to a single task. Isolated workspace.
- **Context Extractor** — your archivist. Watches Raw/ notes, classifies and distills them for agent consumption. Runs continuously.

## Autonomy Progression

Agents earn scope through demonstrated reliability:
1. **Observer** — read-only, learning the entity context
2. **Contributor** — can write to designated areas (Distilled/, Logs/)
3. **Operator** — can spawn sub-agents and manage workflows
4. **Autonomous** — full trust, self-directed within northstar boundaries

- Strategic decisions align to a single source of truth

## Gamification Hooks
- [ ] Northstar clarity score: how specific and actionable is the vision → progression from vague to laser-focused
- [ ] Agent alignment rate: % of agent actions that trace back to northstar goals → higher = more autonomy earned
- [ ] Update frequency: how often the human refines the northstar → active engagement signal
